# 💫 About Me:
I am an MSc graduate in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science from Keele University, with a background in Computer Science and Engineering from College of Engineering, Guindy. My work focuses on designing intelligent systems that solve real-world problems, including phishing detection, personalized music recommendation, demand forecasting, face recognition, and handwriting recognition. Through my academic and project experience, I have developed AI-driven solutions with measurable impact, such as high-recall phishing detection models and highly accurate recommendation systems. I am particularly interested in applying Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to industrial systems, cybersecurity, and large-scale automation.
